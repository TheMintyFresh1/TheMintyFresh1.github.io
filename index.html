About Me

Hello, I’m Becca Kingham! I discovered my passion for data and code when I was very young, and I have taken that fascination all the way to pursuing a degree in Computational Data Science at Michigan State University. I love the process of transforming raw numbers into meaningful results, whether through exploring large datasets or creating informative visualizations. I’m skilled in Python and R, but Excel is my go-to for detailed analysis and reporting. One of my favorite projects has been analyzing telemetry data from my father’s Formula car, where I help optimize performance and racing strategies using Race Studio 3. When I’m not deep in data, you’ll often find me jumping with my favorite horses.
